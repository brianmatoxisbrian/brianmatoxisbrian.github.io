History

White Water Rafting: A Journey Through Waves Established: 2022 Founders: Brian Matomola and Gabs Nyovest Headquarters: Aaub Dam Brief History:
In the radiant summer of 2022, two adventure enthusiasts, Brian Matomola and Gabs Nyovest, shared a thrilling rafting journey on the challenging waters near Aaub Dam. Their shared enthusiasm and vision for bringing an unparalleled rafting experience to the area led to the birth of White Water Rafting. Starting humbly with just a few rafts and a dream, Brian and Gabs, through their dedication to safety, adventure, and sustainability, quickly carved a niche for White Water Rafting in the adventure tourism industry. Within a short span, their innovative offerings, like sunrise rafting and eco-adventure packages, set White Water apart. Their emphasis on blending thrill with environmental consciousness attracted both locals and tourists alike. As they moved into 2023, the duo launched specialized courses in rafting, catering to schools, and introducing unique corporate team-building events on the water. Their unwavering commitment to eco-friendly practices and community involvement has positioned White Water Rafting as a frontrunner in sustainable adventure tourism. Brian and Gabs, while still deeply involved, have expanded their team, bringing in experts from around the world. Yet, their initial vision remains unchanged. White Water Rafting, though young, promises a blend of exhilaration, safety, and commitment to the environment, making it a sought-after destination for rafting enthusiasts.
